Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 18, 2026  ·  Modular Technology Solutions Pte Ltd, Singapore

This Privacy Policy explains how Modular Technology Solutions Pte Ltd ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use Startup Staff. We are committed to handling your data with transparency and respect.

01 — Who we are

Startup Staff is operated by Modular Technology Solutions Pte Ltd, a company incorporated in Singapore. If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at help@modulartechnologysolutions.com.

Startup Staff is a client-side web application. Most of the service runs directly in your browser. We use Supabase (based in the US) as our database and authentication backend, and AI responses are generated by third-party AI providers using API keys you supply.

02 — What information we collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service. The following table summarises the categories of data we hold:

Data Source Purpose Where stored
Name, email, profile photo LinkedIn OAuth Identity & authentication Supabase Auth
Founder name, startup description, HQ country Onboarding form Personalising AI responses Supabase DB
AI provider API keys Settings modal Making LLM API calls on your behalf Supabase DB (encrypted at rest)
Staff customisations & personas Customise modal Building specialist system prompts Supabase DB
Knowledge base content (URLs, uploaded files) Staff KB modal Grounding AI responses in your context Supabase DB
Memory (default & session) Memory panel / auto-generated Maintaining context across sessions Supabase DB
Model assignments per staff member Settings modal Routing queries to the right AI model Supabase DB
Onboarding skip preference Onboarding flow Not showing onboarding on repeat sign-in Supabase DB

We do not collect your chat messages or conversation history on our servers. Chat history is held only in your browser's memory for the duration of your session and is cleared on page reload.

03 — How we use your information

We use the information we collect exclusively to provide and improve Startup Staff. Specifically:

  • Your LinkedIn profile information is used solely to identify you and associate your settings with your account.
  • Your founder preferences and startup description are injected into AI system prompts to make responses relevant to your context.
  • Your API keys are used to make requests to AI providers on your behalf when you submit a query. They are never shared with any party other than the AI provider you have configured.
  • Your knowledge base content is included in specialist system prompts when relevant, so your AI team has the context it needs to help you.
  • Your memory entries (default and session) are included in AI prompts to maintain continuity across sessions.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service.

04 — AI providers and third parties

When you submit a query, your messages — along with your system prompt context — are sent directly from your browser to the AI provider's API using your own API key. We do not proxy or log these requests.

The AI providers you may configure include:

  • OpenAI (openai.com) — subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (anthropic.com) — subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini (ai.google.dev) — subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Mistral AI (mistral.ai) — subject to Mistral's Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI-compatible endpoints — subject to the policy of the provider you configure

Each provider's privacy policy governs how they handle the content of your API calls. We encourage you to review the relevant policies before use.

We use Supabase as our database and authentication provider. Your account data is stored on Supabase infrastructure, subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy. Supabase is SOC 2 Type 2 certified and stores data in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

05 — Data retention

Your account data (founder preferences, API keys, staff customisations, knowledge base, and memory) is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete or update any of this data at any time through the application's Settings and Memory panels.

If you wish to have your account and all associated data deleted, contact us at help@modulartechnologysolutions.com and we will process the deletion within 14 days.

Chat history is never persisted to our servers — it exists only in your browser session and is lost when you close or reload the page.

06 — Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Data at rest in Supabase is encrypted by default.
  • Authentication is handled via LinkedIn OAuth — we never see or store your LinkedIn password.
  • Row-level security (RLS) in Supabase ensures that each user can only access their own data.

Note on API keys: Your API keys are loaded into the application at sign-in and held in browser memory for use during your session. Any JavaScript running on the page — including browser extensions — could theoretically access this memory. We recommend using API keys with usage limits set at your provider's dashboard, and revoking keys if you suspect any compromise.

07 — Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, port, or delete your personal data. We honour these rights for all users regardless of location. To exercise any of these rights:

  • Access or correct your data directly within the application via Settings, the Memory panel, and the Customise modal.
  • Delete specific data (knowledge base content, memory, staff customisations) directly within the application.
  • Request full account deletion by emailing help@modulartechnologysolutions.com.

We do not sell personal data. We do not share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We will not disclose your data to law enforcement without a valid legal order, and we will notify you when permitted by law.

08 — Cookies and local storage

Startup Staff uses browser localStorage to persist your theme preference and certain UI state (founder name, startup description, HQ country) locally on your device. These are not cookies and are not transmitted to our servers — they are convenience caches that survive page reloads.

Supabase may set session cookies to maintain your authenticated state. These are strictly necessary for the service to function and are not used for tracking or advertising.

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising cookies.

09 — Children

Startup Staff is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10 — Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email to your registered address or via an in-app notice. The "Effective date" at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
